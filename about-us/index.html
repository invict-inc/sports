Who are TANZANIA WHEELCHAIR BASKETBALL ASSOCIATION

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND OF TWBA


TWBA has been established and registered by the National Sports Council of Tanzania with a registration number NSC 12237 dated 18th December, 2018.

The main objective of establishing TWBA is to give persons with disabilities the opportunity to participate and take part in both leisure and competitive sports events and develop the sport to the international level.

After its establishment, in 2019 TWBA managed to recruit a good number of athletes who train at JK Sports Park. In same year TWBA managed to send two teams composed with male and female prayers to the East African Zone FIVE Wheelchair Basketball Championship held at Kasarani Stadium in Kenya








Mission:

mission of TANZANIA WHEELCHAIR BASKETBALL ASSOCIATION is to push growth of wheelchair basketball sport countrywide while boosting teamwork with local and International sports associations and clubs.

Vision:

Vision of TANZANIA WHEELCHAIR BASKETBALL ASSOCIATION is to ensure pathways exist for the continuity and stability of development of sports for persons with a disability in Tanzania.

Goals:

To provide clubs, teams, centres and Individual athletes with the skills to develop the sport in their own territory in a sustainable, structured, progressive and professional manner.

To impart, through educational strategies, the required knowledge to developing clubs, teams, centres and Individual athletes in order to ensure the long-term sustainable development of disability sports.

To improve the technical level of our coaches and match officials by providing technical assistance

To improve the quality of human resources needed to better market and administer sports for persons with a disability

To motivate and increase the participation of women at all levels and to improve the technical level of women competitors.

To motivate and provide the youth with a structured pathway for development through sports
